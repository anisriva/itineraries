Andaman trip 2023

Day 1 (13th Nov): Departure from Bangalore & Arrival at Port Blair

Early Morning:

  • 06:50: Book a cab and leave home for Bangalore airport.

Morning:

  • 10:50: Depart from Bangalore.
  • Arrive at Veer Savarkar International Airport, Port Blair.
  • Take the cab ride to Sea Shell Coral Cove.

Afternoon:

  • Check-in and unwind.
  • Enjoy a relaxed lunch at the hotel.

Evening:

  • Short drive to Cellular Jail for the Light and Sound show.
  • Dinner Options: Refer to the restaurant list for Port Blair.

Day 2 (14th Nov): Havelock Island

Morning:

  • Breakfast at Sea Shell Coral Cove.
  • 09:30: Book a cab for a 20-minute ride to Haddo Jetty.
  • 11:00: Reach jetty (ensure you're 30 minutes early).
  • 11:30: Depart for Havelock Island via ferry.

Afternoon:

  • 13:00: Arrive at Havelock.
  • Take a short 5-minute cab ride to Sea Shell Havelock and check-in.
  • Lunch Options: At the hotel or refer to the restaurant list for Havelock.

Evening:

  • Explore the property, nearby beaches, and unwind.
  • Dinner Options: Refer to the restaurant list for Havelock.

Day 3 (15th Nov): Havelock Island Exploration

Morning:

  • Breakfast at Sea Shell Havelock.
  • Book local transport to Elephant Beach, the top spot for water sport activities. Ensure to pre-book these activities.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch Options: Refer to the restaurant list for Havelock.

Evening:

  • Visit Kalapathar beach or stroll around nearby beaches.
  • Dinner Options: Refer to the restaurant list for Havelock.

Day 4 (16th Nov): Havelock Island Exploration

Morning:

  • Early breakfast at Sea Shell Havelock.
  • Book local transport to visit Radhanagar beach.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch Options: Refer to the restaurant list for Havelock.
  • Visit Kalapathar beach or stroll around nearby beaches.

Evening:

  • Dinner Options: Refer to the restaurant list for Havelock.

Day 5 (17th Nov): Return to Port Blair & Departure to Bangalore

Early Morning:

  • Breakfast at Sea Shell Havelock.
  • 08:00: Take a short cab ride to Havelock Jetty.
  • 08:30: Reach jetty (ensure you're 30 minutes early).
  • 09:00: Depart for Port Blair via ferry.

Morning:

  • 11:00: Arrive at Port Blair.
  • 11:30: Visit local market or souvenir shops.

Afternoon:

  • 14:50: Depart from Veer Savarkar International Airport, Port Blair.
  • 19:10: Arrive at Bangalore airport.
  • Book a cab for the ride home.

Notable places to eat:

Port Blair:

  • New Lighthouse Restaurant: 9/10 (Renowned for its seafood and Indian specialties with a sea view.)
  • The Islander Restaurant: 8.5/10 (Good ambiance with a variety of cuisines, ranging from Indian to international.)
  • Annapurna Restaurant: 8/10 (Vegetarian-friendly, serving both North and South Indian dishes.)
  • Hotel Kattabomman: 7.5/10 (Known for its authentic South Indian dishes, offering a traditional dining experience.)

Havelock:

  • Something Different – A Beachside Café: 9.5/10 (Great ambiance with beach views and a versatile menu.)
  • Full Moon Café: 9/10 (Laid-back ambiance with an array of seafood options.)
  • Red Snapper at Wild Orchid Resort: 9/10 (Excellent seafood options in a resort ambiance.)
  • B3 – At Barefoot Scuba: 9/10 (Popular for breakfast and caters to the scuba-diving crowd.)
  • Bonova Cafe And Pub: 8.5/10 (Good for evening relaxation with a variety of drinks and snacks.)
  • Shanaya's Kitchen & Bar: 8/10 (Offers a mix of Indian and international cuisines.)
  • Ocean Prime - Multicuisine Restaurant: 8/10 (Variety of cuisines in a good ambiance.)
  • BLACK Pepper Lounge: 8/10 (Good Indian and seafood options.)

Notes:

  1. Double-check the operational hours of eateries. Reservations might be needed for restaurants like "Something Different - A Beachside Café".
